FAQ

How to play Pick Me/Pick the Building?
In 60 seconds, correctly identify the building captured/highlighted in each of the images shown, as many as you can. Click the selection box to scroll and select or type to search among the provided options. 1 point per correct answer. Images are randomly selected, and no repeats until all have been shown.

How to play Click Me/Click the Buidling?
In 60 seconds, click on where you think a specific building is on a partial UBC campus map and get as many locations correct as you can. 1 point per correct answer. The building being asked is randomly selected (from a larger pool than Pick Me), and no repeats until all have been asked.

What was the source of the images/Where can I get cheat sheets for the challenges?
UBC Campus Maps + Wayfinding is where a base map was downloaded and edited to give the images used on this site. You are welcome to explore and appreciate the shapes of all UBC architecture before starting the challenges, but no cheat sheets :) .

What does UBC stand for?
